About Iron Man

Iron Man, also known as Tony Stark, is a genius billionaire inventor who became a superhero after building a powerful suit of armor. With his unmatched intelligence and tech skills, he fights evil and protects the world as Iron Man.

Introduced in Marvel Comics in 1963 and portrayed by Robert Downey Jr. in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, Iron Man became a symbol of courage, redemption, and innovation.

His iconic quote: “I am Iron Man.” lives on as one of the most powerful moments in superhero history.
